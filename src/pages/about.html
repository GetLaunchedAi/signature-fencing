Who We Are

Built for Installers. Trusted by Homeowners.

At Signature Fencing, we specialize in premium vinyl and aluminum fencing systems engineered for real-world performance. Every product we offer, from fabricated panels to welded gates is shaped by decades of hands-on installation experience and refined by a deep understanding of what contractors, property owners, and distributors actually need on-site.

We believe quality starts long before a fence is installed. That’s why we’ve brought fabrication, welding, powder coating, and packaging under one roof, giving us total control over the consistency, fit, and durability of every system we deliver. Whether you're building a residential backyard enclosure or a multi-property commercial fence line, we make sure each component arrives installer-ready and built to last.

Our team is made up of professionals who’ve spent years in the field—not just on the factory floor. That perspective drives everything we do, from gate alignment and fastener selection to packaging that prevents damage and speeds up unloading. We're not just manufacturers, we’re problem solvers who design fencing with real-world challenges in mind.

Founded on the principle that quality work should be simple to install and built to perform, Signature Fencing continues to grow as a trusted partner for contractors, wholesalers, and homeowners who value precision, speed, and long-term reliability.
